Frequently Asked Questions

Why are coaching businesses considered high-risk by payment processors?
Coaching businesses often deal with high-ticket sales, recurring billing, and digital products, which can lead to higher chargeback rates and refund requests, making them high-risk for payment processors.
How can I reduce chargebacks in my coaching business?
Implement clear refund policies, provide excellent customer support, use secure payment gateways with fraud prevention tools, and ensure transparent communication with clients.
What should I look for in a high-risk payment processor?
Seek processors with experience in the coaching industry, high approval rates, robust fraud protection, flexible payment options, and transparent pricing.
