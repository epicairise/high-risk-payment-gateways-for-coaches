Frequently Asked Questions

Why do coaching programs get flagged by Stripe?

Coaching and transformation programs often trigger compliance alerts due to refund risk, lack of clear deliverables, or industry perception as "bizop-like" offers.

Can I sell high-ticket coaching with Stripe?

Technically yes, but you're at risk of account shutdown. If you sell $1,000+ offers, use a high-risk merchant account or a split processor.

What qualifies as a "high-risk" coaching offer?

Anything with high price tags, no physical product, refund guarantees, or claims related to income or personal transformation.
